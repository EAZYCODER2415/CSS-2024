About Us

PROJECT EXPLANATION

Chiang Mai Scholar's Sustainability(CSS) is a club that focuses on the sustainability of the environment. We will recycle and clean the trash and will set up trash bins around the school campus (for our club members or people who want to help) to throw clean trash that we can use straight away and ultimately turn them into roadblocks for underprivileged communities. It will be a rewarding way to earn service hours whilst making a difference within the CMIS community and areas across the nation in need. Furthermore, we will provide fun activities and events for further education on recycling, and implentation of roadblocks.
